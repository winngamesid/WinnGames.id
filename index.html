License Activation

  1. Launch Steam and log in to your account
  2. Click on "Add a Game" in the bottom left corner
  3. Select "Activate a Product on Steam"
  4. Click "Next" and agree to the subscriber agreement
  5. Enter your product code exactly as provided
  6. Follow any additional prompts to complete activation

Tip: License keys are case-sensitive. Copy and paste to avoid errors.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

  • "Invalid Product Code" - Verify you entered the code correctly
  • "Product Already Owned" - Check your game library
  • "Installation Failed" - Check disk space and permissions
  • "Game Not Launching" - Update graphics drivers

Advanced Solutions:

  1. Clear Steam download cache
  2. Verify integrity of game files
  3. Reinstall Steam client
  4. Contact support with error details
